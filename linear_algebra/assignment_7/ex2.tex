\section*{Exercise 2}

Evaluate the following determinats using the minor expansion method. 

\subsection*{Part A}

\[
	\genericmat{v}{
		4&0&0&5\\
		1&7&2&-5\\
		3&0&0&0\\
		8&3&1&7
	} = 
	3 \cdot 
	\genericmat{v}{
		0&0&5\\
		7&2&-5\\
		3&1&7
	} = 
	3 \cdot (35 - 30) = 15
\]

\subsection*{Part B}

\[
	\begin{aligned}
	\genericmat{v}{
		3 & 0 & 0 & 0\\
 		7 & -2 & 0 & 0\\
 		2 & 6 & 3 & 0\\
 		3 & -8 & 4 & -3
	} &= 3 \cdot
	\genericmat{v}{
		-2 & 0 & 0 \\
		6 & 3 & 0 \\
		-8 & 4 & -3
	} \\
	&=
	3 \cdot (18 + 0 + 0 - 0 - 0 - 0) \\
	&= 54
	\end{aligned}
\]
